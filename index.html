Our Story

Freshly Baked,
Taste the Difference

At Sweet&Salt, we bring a slice of Parisian patisserie to Lincoln Park. Every morning, our bakers arrive before dawn to craft each pastry, cake, and loaf by hand — using time-honored European techniques and the finest ingredients.

From our signature sachertorte to warm, buttery croissants and perfectly pulled espressos, every detail is designed to make your day a little sweeter (and saltier).

  • European-trained pastry chefs
  • Locally sourced, premium ingredients
  • All-day breakfast & specialty coffee
  • Cozy neighborhood atmosphere
